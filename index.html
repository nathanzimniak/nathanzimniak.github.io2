I am a PhD student working at IPAG in the SHERPAS team (Sources of High Energy, Relativistic Plasmas, Accretion Structures). My research focuses on accretion discs and the outflows emitted by them. Such structures are observed around protostars, binary systems or black holes.


The aim of my work is to improve the understanding of these "accretion-ejection" structures. For this purpose, I model them by using magnetohydrodynamics equations (MHD), obtained by combining fluid dynamics and Maxwell's equations.
